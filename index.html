Our Roots

Made from what grows around us

Mann & Leaf began with something close to home: coconut trees, skilled hands in the family, and the belief that natural materials can still make strong everyday products. We started by turning what our village already knows well into useful items for modern homes, beginning with the coconut broom.

By working with local families in our region, we source renewable raw materials like fallen coconut leaves, golden jute, seasoned bamboo, and local clay. We focus on simple crafting methods that preserve the natural qualities of the materials, making them durable enough for daily household tasks.

"We believe in simple utility, long-lasting natural materials, and supporting the village artisans who handcraft each item."

— The Team at Mann & Leaf

The Difference

Why homes choose Mann & Leaf

Simple, reliable, and sustainable everyday household utility crafts.

Small-Batch Production

Every item is individually hand-bound by skilled village artisans in Tamil Nadu, preserving traditional family skills.

Natural Material Based

We source clean, renewable raw materials directly from local plantations, including coconut midribs, coir, golden jute, bamboo, and clay.

Useful Daily Products

These are functional, robust household tools designed specifically for heavy, everyday sweeping, storing, and kitchen utility.

Cleaner, Simpler Packaging

We avoid plastic wraps. All items are packed using simple paper wraps and natural ropes, reducing packaging waste.

Village Sourced

Sourcing directly from rural workshops ensures fair incomes for family artisans, helping sustain local village economies.

Good for Resellers

The Journey

From Tree to Hearth

How simple raw materials become finished, useful household tools.

01

1. Raw Sourcing

Sourcing raw coconut leaf midribs sustainably from local family plantations in Tamil Nadu.

02

2. Hand Prep

Leaves are stripped of their green blades and sun-dried for strength and flexibility.

03

3. Craft Binding

Midribs are bundled and tightly hand-bound using natural coconut coir braids for a strong, secure grip.

04

4. Home Utility

The finished broom is bundled, checked, and packed for everyday home use.
